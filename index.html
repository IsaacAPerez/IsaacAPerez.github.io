TeamUp: Find Your Game. Published iOS app.

TeamUp was made to create a community that promotes physical and mental wellness in a fun and easy way. This app allows people to connect with others to engage in various sports. Users can see posts around their current location and also create post. TeamUp took an minimalistic approach to help users view,create, and connect with others.

T-PREP Transfer Pre-Engineering Program

Summer program that allowed me to get a head start in my education at Cal. I was able to connect with other incoming engineering students and faculty. We were organized into teams where we were tasked to create a product that was aimed to combat vital issues around the world. My team and I created Xpired where our goal was to combat food waste on a large scale through small scale applications.
